From the Looms of Anatolia

To the heart of your home

Some things are made to be used once. Then there are the others — the ones woven to outlast the hands that made them. bkCAMUR Home was built around that second kind.

Every vintage Turkish rug we carry was handwoven — often decades ago — by artisans in Anatolia using hand-spun wool, natural dyes, and techniques passed down through generations of weavers. Our kilim pillow covers are cut and crafted from those same antique kilims: upcycled, sustainable, and one-of-a-kind. No two are identical. That's the point.

Our hand-embroidered suzani pillow covers bring a different tradition into the mix — the Central Asian art of silk-thread embroidery, traditionally stitched by brides as part of a dowry. The bold florals and hours of hand-stitching carry real history in every thread.

We bring these pieces to the United States so that design lovers, interior decorators, collectors, and anyone who believes a home should have soul can find them without flying to Istanbul.

— The bkCAMUR Home team, Philadelphia
